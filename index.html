About SehatGuide AI

Intelligent Health Assistant

SehatGuide AI is a smart healthcare chatbot designed to provide fast and reliable guidance on symptoms, preventive care, and vaccination schedules. The system simplifies health information into clear, easy-to-follow advice for everyone.

Community Support

Focused on bridging healthcare gaps in rural and semi-urban areas. Users receive timely updates, preventive care tips, and outbreak alerts customized to their location. This ensures people stay informed even without frequent access to doctors or hospitals.

Vaccination & Telemedicine

Users can discover nearby vaccination centers, check immunization schedules, search for doctors by specialty and city, and access free telemedicine services. SehatGuide AI empowers communities to take proactive care of their health easily and safely.
